Huginn & Muninn — Scenario Gallery

20 real-world conspiracy and misinformation claims, analyzed through a 6-agent AI pipeline

Each scenario asks three questions: What legitimate need does this claim serve? What manipulation techniques are being used, and by whom? And where is the common ground between people who believe this and those who don’t? The goal is not to dismiss people who hold these beliefs, but to understand them well enough to build bridges across the divide.
